Privacy

What we hold, and what we do not.

Last updated 30 June 2026

darzi exists to bring a private craft into a considered space. That trust begins with restraint — we collect only what a commission requires, and nothing it does not.

What we collect

When you create an account and request an appointment through the darzi app, we collect the details needed to recognise you and to arrange and honour your visit:

Your name
So the atelier knows who they are attending.
Email address
To create and secure your account, and to send you the code that signs you in.
Mobile number
To confirm your appointment and reach you about your commission.
Address
So a master tailor can attend your home for the visit.
Booking details
The service requested, your chosen atelier, and the date and time of your appointment.

How we use it

Your information is used to arrange your appointment, to let your chosen atelier prepare for the visit, and to keep you informed about your commission. The atelier receives only what it needs to attend you — your name, your general area, and the appointment time. Your full address is held in the app as the record of the booking; it is not shared in messages.

Where it is held

Your booking is stored securely with Supabase, our database provider. Appointment notifications are sent to the atelier through Twilio, which carries the message on our behalf. These providers process your information solely to deliver the service, under their own data protections, and not for any purpose of their own.

What we do not do

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with advertisers. darzi does not use your details to market unrelated products to you, and places no tracking on you beyond what the booking itself requires. Discretion is the point of darzi, not a setting you have to find. Should any of this ever change, we will say so plainly and ask your permission first.

Keeping and removing your information

We hold your details for as long as you keep an account and a record of your commissions with us. If you would like your information removed, write to us and we will delete it, save for anything we are required to retain.

Your control

You may ask us at any time what we hold about you, request a correction, or ask us to delete it. We will answer in good faith and without friction.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as darzi grows or as the law requires. If we make a material change to how your information is used, we will tell you — within the app or by writing to you — before that change takes effect, so the choice to continue remains yours.

Contact

For anything concerning your privacy, or to make a request about your information, write to darzi.pk1@gmail.com.
